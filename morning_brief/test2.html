A number of countries and U.S. states are asking questions about international agreements that will give new pandemic powers to the World Health Organization. One of the main concerns is that the WHO will have authority to declare a “health emergency,” making it the coordinating authority for the world’s public health response. The U.N. organization is ramping up efforts to persuade member countries to get in line before its annual meeting May 27 to June 1.

White House Pushed to Soften Robert Hur’s Report on Biden’s ‘Poor Memory’

Millions of dollars’ worth of COVID-19 ventilators that the Canadian government bought in a purchase the prime minister described as “innovative” wound up being sold as scrap metal for as little as $6 apiece. The government initially bought the machines for over $22,000 each before find out that elderly patients didn’t do well on ventilators.

Father Asks Court to Stop 27-Year-Old Daughter’s MAID Death, Review Doctors’ Sign-Off

A Calgary father wants to stop his autistic daughter’s MAID death, arguing she suffers from mental illness. The 27-year-old was scheduled to be killed Feb. 1 but her father received an injunction and now wants her application reviewed.
